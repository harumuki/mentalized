<p>The velium helmets have the possibility to be customized via a quest, making for a very unique look. I will try to continually
update this page when I locate better images.</p>

<!--more-->

<p>Please note, that these images are with pre-Luclin graphics. The new Luclin-graphics uses these helmet models for many of their helmets.
I am not entirely certain how customized helmets look with Luclin-graphics enabled.</p>
