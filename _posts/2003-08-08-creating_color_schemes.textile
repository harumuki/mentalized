For the design-challenged of us, color scheme creators can be a great boon when trying to design websites where the colors don't look totally out of sync with each other. 

Pick a base color and these tools suggest colors that go with it:

* "ColorSchemer Online (ColorSchemer Online)":http://www.colorschemer.com/online.html 
* "QuickColor (KOHAISTYLE.com: QuickColor)":http://kohaistyle.com/scripts/quickcolor/ (Requires Flash)
* "ColorMatch 5K (ColorMatch 5K)":http://www.colormatch.dk/ (Requires IE it seems)
* "+Color scheme+(Pixy's Color schemer)":http://www.pixy.cz/apps/barvy/index-en.html
* "+ColorMatch Remix+(ColorMatch Remix)":http://color.twysted.net/ +(Improved version of ColorMatch 5K)+
* "+The colour wizard+(The colour wizard)":http://www.webwhirlers.com/colors/wizard.asp
* "+EasyRGB+(EasyRGB)":http://www.easyrgb.com/harmonies.php
