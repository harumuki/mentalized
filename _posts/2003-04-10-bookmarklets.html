Bookmarklets are extra browser features that you can store as bookmarks on your personal toolbar.

www.squarefree.com/bookmarklets is a collection of some great bookmarklets. Most the Web Development ones have made it into my personal toolbar and I've already solved one CSS problem using them - excellent aid, even for non developers.
